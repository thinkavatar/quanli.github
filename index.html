I am a tenure-track assistant professor at School of Information Science and Technology, ShanghaiTech University. I was a Research Scientist at WeBank AI Group. I received my Ph.D at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology Computer Science and Engineering, where my supervisors are Prof. Xiaojuan Ma and Prof. Huamin Qu.

Before joining HKUST, I was working in NetEase Games, a leading Chinese game operator. I obtained my master degree from Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Li Chen, and bachelor degree from Wuhan University, and got another bachelor degree in English Literature from School of Foreign Language at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research focuses on the areas of Visualization, Visual Analytics, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and Data Science. I develop advanced interaction and visualization techniques that promote the interplay between humans, machines, and data. My research aims to boost data analysis and mining efficiency that involves large-scale data, complex applications and machine learning models, forming “human-in-the-loop”.
