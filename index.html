Quan Li

I am a tenure-track assistant professor at School of Information Science and Technology, ShanghaiTech University, also affiliated with Fintech Initiative and HCI Initiative, SIST, ShanghaiTech University.

I received my Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where my supervisors are Prof. Xiaojuan Ma and Prof. Huamin Qu. Before joining HKUST, I was a senior researcher at NetEase Games, a leading Chinese game operator. I obtained my master's degree from Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Li Chen, and bachelor's degree from Wuhan University. I have another bachelor's degree in English Literature at School of Foreign Language, Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Before joining ShanghaiTech, I was a senior research scientist at WeBank AI Group.

We welcome undergraduate/graduate students, post-docs, research assistants, and visiting interns/scholars. Please send me your CV if you are interested in these topics or have any question.

Research

My research focuses on the areas of Big Data Visualization and Visual Analytics, Data Science, Interpretable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), Data Storytelling, Human-Computer Interaction, and Computational Social Science. I develop advanced interaction and visual analytics techniques that promote the interplay between humans, machines, and data, aiming to boost the efficiency of real-world data analytical activities that involve a large amount of data, various complicated models, and a diverse group of users with different backgrounds. I contribute to the design, development, and evaluation of visualizations that support the general data science workflow: from Interactive Data Exploration, then to Model Development and Interpretation, and finally to Insight Communication and Storytelling.
